SmileCraft

This is a demo. Emoji from Twitter have been processed into series of numbers called vectors. Similar emoji have similar series of numbers.
Type emoji abbreviations to perform arithmetic and find the nearest emoji not in the input. Repeat emoji to subtract or multiply, or use a number to divide.
The images represent values, with brighter blue being more positive and brighter red being more negative.
Learn more about word vectors, or view a visualization of the emoji space.
